About Me

My mission as a designer is to solve the users problem before creating something that just looks pretty.

As a User Experience student, I aim to put myself in the users’ shoes to create a product that serves their needs and is enjoyable to use. Using Design-Thinking and Agile Methods to identify and solve problems, I enjoy the collaboration with other creative minds and learn from their experience and perspective.

Teamwork

Having worked with a lot of different departments, I became an ambassador for the importance of Design, sharing my knowledge about tools and standards as well as user perspectives and best practices. I also gained a lot of insights from the marketing perspective and especially enjoyed working with developers. It helped me gain an understanding of how the product we are designing works and what the limitations and the possibilities are.

Currently I am looking for a position as a UX/UI Designer to extend my knowledge and grow within an experienced team.
